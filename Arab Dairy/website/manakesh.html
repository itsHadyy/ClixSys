Manakesh

ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tablespoon yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water, as needed
  • 4 tablespoons thyme
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 pieces of Panda Gourmet triangles

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, yeast, salt, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the oil and gradually start adding warm water while kneading.
  3. Knead for 7 minutes until the dough becomes smooth.
  4. Leave the dough for an hour.
  5. Divide the dough into 8 equal parts.
  6. Cover and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  7. Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and flatten each dough ball into a flat circle about Â¼ inch thick.
  8. Mix the olive oil with thyme and add Panda Gourmet triangles.
  9. Transfer the dough circles to a baking sheet, spread the thyme oil mixture over the dough, and add Panda Gourmet cheese triangles. Cover and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  10. Bake in a preheated oven at 200 degrees Celsius until golden brown then serve hot.
