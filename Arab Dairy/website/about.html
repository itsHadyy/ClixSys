Welcome to
Arab Dairy's Family

Arab Dairy was established in 1985 as a shareholding company, with production starting in 1990. We offer a wide range of high-quality dairy products, including:

Processed Cheese       Swiss Cheese       Mozzarella Cheese       White Cheese

Our commitment to quality is demonstrated at every stage of the production process. This commitment includes thorough testing of raw materials and continuous quality checks during manufacturing. Compliance with ISO 9001-2000 and HACCP procedures ensures the safety and quality of all our dairy products.

Vision

To become the leading producer of healthy dairy products, committed to quality, innovation, sustainability and consumer satisfaction.
